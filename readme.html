Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how MathWarriors (the "App") protects the privacy of its users and complies with Google Play’s User Data policy. Our commitment is to safeguard all user data and ensure compliance with applicable regulations.

About Us

Collected Data

MathWarriors does not collect, access, or store any personal or sensitive user data.

Advertising and Third Parties

The App uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect certain non-personal data to serve ads effectively. For details on how AdMob collects and handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children Under 13

The App is designed for all age groups, including children. To protect children’s privacy, MathWarriors does not collect or store personal information. Google AdMob complies with child-directed ad policies under COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act).

Data Sharing

The App does not share any user data with third parties. Any data processed by AdMob is subject to Google’s privacy policies.

Data Retention and Deletion Policy

Since no user data is collected, stored, or processed by MathWarriors, there is no data retention or deletion applicable.
